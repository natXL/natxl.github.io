Where to Go in Hawaii

Kona (West Side): Great for sunny weather, beaches, snorkeling, and shopping.

Hilo (East Side): Ideal for lush rainforests, waterfalls, and exploring.

Must-Do Activities

1. Visit a Volcano

See Kīlauea and Mauna Loa volcanoes. Hike trails like Kīlauea Iki Crater or Thurston Lava Tube.

2. Snorkeling and Water Adventures

Captain Cook's Cove: Take a boat ride and snorkel at the Captain Cook Monument.

3. Beaches

Hapuna Beach: White sand, great for swimming or just relaxing on the beach.

Fine Dining & Sunset Views: There are lots of fancy places to eat and watch the sunset!

4. Telescope

Mauna Kea Summit: If the weather is good, Andy (My brother-in-law) can take us on a behind-the-scenes tour of the Canada-France-Hawaii Telescope (CFHT).
