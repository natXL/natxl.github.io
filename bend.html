Angel & Ed’s 2025 Bend, Oregon Getaway

Trip Itinerary; June 19, June 20, June 21, June 22

🏡 Where we stayed

Nestled in pines just outside Bend, the custom-built Airbnb featured vaulted ceilings, a private hot tub, forest wildlife visits, and sweeping mountain views.

📅 Trip Itinerary

June 19 – Rancher Butcher Chef - Dinner with Adrianna & Paul

Upscale farm‑to‑table dinner with premium beef and creative cocktails in Northwest Crossing. The Source named it “Restaurant of the Year” 2024

June 20 Morning – ROAM Brunch

Farm‑fresh brunch classics (Eggs Benedict, Bloody Marys) in a relaxed downtown setting

June 20 – High Desert Museum

Explored wildlife, history, and Native American culture on a 135‑acre campus

June 20 Afternoon – Deschutes Brewery & Co.

Tours and tastings at Bend’s original brewpub since 1988, 19 rotating taps, games, and beer‑centric dishes

June 20 Dinner – LaRosa

Modern Mexican fare with fresh ingredients and vibrant cocktails.

June 21 Morning – Tumalo Falls Hike

Scenic 4‑mile hike to a 97‑ft waterfall through pine forests and basalt cliffs in Deschutes National Forest

June 21 Lunch – Cotto

Rustic post‑hike Italian pasta and wine in a cozy downtown restaurant.

June 21 Afternoon – Dogwood at the Pine Shed

Inventive craft cocktails in a rustic, artsy lodge atmosphere.

June 21 Dinner – Pisano’s Woodfired Pizza

Hand‑tossed wood‑fired pizzas with fresh, local toppings.

June 22 Brunch – Victorian Cafe

Classic brunch favorites and towering Bloody Marys—perfect ending to the Bend weekend.
