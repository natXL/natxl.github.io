Angel & Ed’s Bend, Oregon Getaway

June 19–22, 2025

🏡 Airbnb Stay

Nestled among pines in Bend, this custom-built Airbnb offered mountain-view decks, a private hot tub under the stars, vaulted ceilings, and serene wildlife glimpses.

📅 Trip Itinerary

June 19 – Rancher Butcher Chef

Farm-to-table dinner led by Chef John Gorham, focusing on premium ranch-raised beef and Spanish-inspired dishes. Located in Northwest Crossing, Bend :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

June 20 Morning – ROAM Brunch

Casual-and-refined brunch downtown, featuring dishes like Eggs Benedict and their signature Bloody Marys. Open 7 AM–2 PM daily :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

June 20 – High Desert Museum

Explored exhibits on regional wildlife, Native American culture, and natural history.

June 20 Afternoon – Deschutes Brewery & The Cellar & Bend Brewing Co.

Brewery hopping: classic craft beers at Deschutes Brewery, a cozy wine flight at The Cellar, and riverside brews at Bend Brewing Company.

June 20 Dinner – LaRosa

Modern Mexican dinner featuring fresh, flavorful dishes and cocktails.

June 21 Morning – Tumalo Falls Hike

7-mile scenic hike to a 97‑ft waterfall in Deschutes National Forest, winding through pine forests and basalt cliffs. Parking requires a Northwest Forest Pass :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

June 21 Lunch – Cotto

Rustic Italian post-hike, pasta and wine in a cozy setting.

June 21 Afternoon – Dogwood at the Pine Shed

Inventive cocktails in a woodsy, artsy atmosphere.

June 21 Dinner – Pisano’s Woodfired Pizza

Delicious, hand-tossed wood-fired pizzas.

June 22 Brunch – Victorian Cafe

Legendary brunch with decadent plates and towering Bloody Marys—spirited send-off to their Bend weekend.
