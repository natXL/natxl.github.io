Angel & Ed’s Bend, Oregon Getaway

June 19–22, 2025

🏡 Airbnb Stay

Nestled among pines in Bend, the custom-built home offered mountain-view decks, a private hot tub under the stars, vaulted ceilings, and serene wildlife glimpses.

📅 Trip Itinerary

June 19 – Rancher Butcher Chef

Farm-to-table dinner focusing on premium ranch-raised beef and creative cocktails.

June 20 Morning – ROAM Brunch

Casual-and-refined brunch featuring farm‑fresh ingredients and signature Bloody Marys.

June 20 – High Desert Museum

Explored exhibits on regional wildlife, Native American culture, and natural history.

June 20 Afternoon – Deschutes Brewery & The Cellar & Bend Brewing Co.

Brewery hopping: Deschutes Brewery, wine at The Cellar, and riverside brews at Bend Brewing Company.

June 20 Dinner – LaRosa

Modern Mexican dinner with fresh, flavorful dishes and cocktails.

June 21 Morning – Tumalo Falls Hike

7‑mile scenic hike to a 97‑ft waterfall in Deschutes National Forest, winding through pine forests and basalt cliffs.

June 21 Lunch – Cotto

Rustic Italian post‑hike, pasta and wine in a cozy setting.

June 21 Afternoon – Dogwood at the Pine Shed

Inventive cocktails in a woodsy, artsy atmosphere.

June 21 Dinner – Pisano’s Woodfired Pizza

Delicious, hand‑tossed wood‑fired pizzas.

June 22 Brunch – Victorian Cafe

Legendary brunch with decadent plates and towering Bloody Marys—perfect farewell to their Bend weekend.
